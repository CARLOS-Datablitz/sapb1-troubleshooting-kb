# SLD GUI:

--- Carpeta de instalación SAP Business One Server ---
En el navegador de la vm(rds o adm): https://files.jezz.systems/sap/ (esta url esta en el Fenruz).
# en el sld:
sld:# cd /usr/sap/SAPBusinessOne/B1_SHF
sld:/usr/sap/SAPBusinessOne/B1_SHF# mkdir 10.0_SP2402HF1   
sld:/usr/sap/SAPBusinessOne/B1_SHF# chown -R b1service0:b1service0 10.0_SP2402HF1/
sld:/usr/sap/SAPBusinessOne/B1_SHF# chomod -R 777 10.0_SP2402HF1/
# en el adm o rds:
unzip la carpeta descargada del files.jezz

--- Intallation and upgrade ----
sld:/usr/sap/SAPBusinessOne/B1_SHF/10.0_SP2402HF1/Package.Linux/ServerComponents > install  ----> Ruta de instalación
sld:/usr/sap/SAPBusinessOne/B1_SHF/10.0_SP2402HF1/Package.Linux/ServerComponents# ./install

--- Ruta de reconfiguration, Validation, Unistallation ----
sld:/usr/sap/SAPBusinessOne > setup
